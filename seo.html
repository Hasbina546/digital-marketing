Digital Marketing Basics

Digital marketing is the promotion of products and services through online platforms. It helps businesses reach a larger audience using the internet.

Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

SEO improves a website's visibility on search engines like Google. Better rankings can increase website traffic and brand awareness.

Social Media Marketing

Social media platforms help businesses connect directly with customers. Regular content sharing can improve engagement and customer loyalty.

Email Marketing

Email marketing is an effective way to communicate with potential and existing customers. Personalized emails can increase conversions and strengthen customer relationships.
